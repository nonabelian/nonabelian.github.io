Quantum Information

Quantum information is a strange, sometimes confusing, yet fundamental area of physics. It connected to many areas of study: computation, AMO, condensed matter, AdS/CFT (entanglement entropy), and simply the fundamentals of quantum mechanics.

My interest in QI is from a holographic point of view. John Preskill's lecture notes and the book by Nielson and Chuang are excellent resources. I hope to post some write-ups and maybe some problems/solutions.
