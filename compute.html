FEniCS

This is a really cool tool for automated differential equation solving. It can handle large systems of time-dependent nonlinear PDEs in up to three spatial demensions, which blows my mind. It offers access to flexible finite element based solvers, with control over the mesh (e.g. adaptive mesh). For many problems, all this can be done in ~100 lines of code! Of course, often to get accurate simulations of difficult problems requires access to a supercomputer.
