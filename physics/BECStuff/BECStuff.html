BEC Stuff

An interesting question, with a large amount of corresponding literature, is: How do we simulate a BEC near the critical point? One quite successful way of answering this question is numerically solving the two-component problem, where one component is the condensate, well described by the Gross-Pitaevski equation, and the other component is the uncondensed atomic gas.
