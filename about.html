About

I program for a living. I have done various consulting gigs, including a mobile computer vision based project using OpenCV and PCL in C++, and image signal analysis in MATLAB. I am currently working on data analysis problems in Python -- the language of choice these days. My interests and work in this area can be found on my GitHub page, and in the Research Interests.

My background is in high energy particle physics. I studied holographic dual models of strongly coupled physics, as can be found in Publications. My research interest in this area has broadened since then, including related work in quantum information, emergent geometry, and tensor networks.

I recently started rock climbing, which is awesome. Not only do you get to experience nature in a unique way, but you are challenged physically and mentally, and you get to tie all sorts of cool knots and hitches! Feel free to check me out on Mountain Project!
