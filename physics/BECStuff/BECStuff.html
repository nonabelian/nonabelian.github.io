BEC Stuff

An interesting question, with a large amount of corresponding literature, is: How do we simulate a BEC near the critical point? One quite successful way of answering this question is numerically solving the two-component problem, where one component is the condensate, well described by the Gross-Pitaevski equation, and the other component is the uncondensed atomic thermal cloud, described by a kinetic equation.

My current interest is in solving the coupled equations for the time evolution of the two-component system.
