Holography

I would like to gather a number of holography related items in this section. Holography, or AdS/CFT, is a conjectured relation between a very strongly coupled conformal field theory in a (d-1)-dimensional spacetime and a weakly coupled asymptotically AdS gravitational theory in d-dimensions. A more loose (or strong) form of the conjecture is simply between a gravitational theory and a field theory on its boundary.

Research in applications of AdS/CFT to various difficult problems is very active. There are many interesting topics and ideas under investigation, and undoubtedly many that are yet to be discovered.
